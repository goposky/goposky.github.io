Devopsdays and other happenings

29 May 2016 on devops, devopsdays, event, amsterdam, meetup

For almost a couple of years I have been following 3 particular 'devopsy' meetup groups in Amsterdam. They offer plenty of opportunity to learn and network, but also a window to the latest buzz in the industry. In no particular order these are:

Devops Amsterdam: A meetup group that was formed by a few engineers in the Amsterdam area, they organize the annual Devopsdays event. Two weeks ago I attended this year's event in Amsterdam. For my actual blog on the event I redirect you here.

Docker Randstad is another meetup group active in the Randstad area that conduct monthly meetups on topics around the docker ecosystem.

Software Circus: They organize a 'fun' tech event annually (software circus), and also hold meetups from time to time in Amsterdam. Their events are themed around Devops and cloud-native technology.
